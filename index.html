Nikolai Chernolutskii

Creating fully responsive websites

I am a web developer whose mission is to bring visual ideas to life through coding. I create fully responsive websites with a clean and semantic code.

With more and more businesses going online, the demand for professionals helping to ensure virtual presence is soaring. I am happy to apply my passion for front end web development towards providing people with websites that are both visually appealing and functional.

How did it all start for me? Well, it’s a dream that I had cherished for a long time but up to a certain moment had never been brave enough to pursue.

Once an English teacher, a translator, a project manager and a tango instructor (which I am still, happily sharing this passion with my wonderful wife), with the start of the pandemic and a sharp drop in tango classes I realized that this was the exact time to turn crisis into an opportunity and seek a new career path, allowing me to help people from all over the world.

Aside from that, it gives me utter pleasure to transform visual ideas created with tools like Figma and the like into the form of code understandable by the web browsers.

At the moment, I am running a website www.site-now.online together with my wife to showcase our work and welcome collaboration opportunities.

Albeit naive, the ultimate goal I’d like to achieve is to assist people in reaching out to their clients, friends or communities, both locally and globally, through making themselves seen on the Web.

In the future I see myself progressing from building websites to single-page applications through various JavaScript frameworks, giving more flexible opportunities to respond to my clients’ needs.

My language skills of English, French and Lithuanian (as well as my native Russian) make verbal communication with the clients easier, whereas my extensive tango background helps to establish non-verbal rapport.
